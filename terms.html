We understand that circumstances change. The following refund schedule applies to all confirmed enrolments:

  • Cancellation 7+ days before course start date: Full refund minus a 10% administrative processing fee.

  • No-show on the first day of class: No refund or credit note will be issued.
  • Withdrawal after course commencement: No refund for sessions already delivered. Remaining sessions may be transferred to another enrolled student with a minimum of 72 hours' written notice.
  • Course cancelled by Gutenberg Languages Institute: Full refund of all fees paid, or an equivalent credit toward a future course, at the student's choice.
