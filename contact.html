Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are your course start dates?

We offer flexible start dates throughout the year. New courses begin every month. Contact us to find the next available start date for your preferred course.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes! We offer both in-person and online classes. Our online courses feature live interactive sessions with the same quality instruction as our in-person programs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, PayPal, and cash payments. Flexible payment plans are available for all courses.

Can I visit the campus before enrolling?

Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to visit our campus. Schedule a tour by calling us or filling out the contact form above.
